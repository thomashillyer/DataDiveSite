About DataDive

DataDive will give students the opportunity to analyze real world datasets and to use their findings in creative and inventive ways. Students will have the opportunity to apply the insights they are able to uncover in any way they see fit, with the opportunity to justify the creation of a software project or to create a case competition style presentation that aims to solve a problem that the data highlights.
