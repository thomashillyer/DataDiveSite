CodeJam: DataDive

November 17th - 19th, 2017

About DataDive

CodeJam: DataDive is a data analysis hackathon which allows students to explore large real world datasets and use their findings in creating unique and inventive software projects. Student teams will have the opportunity to choose to participate in one of the three categories Sports, Healthcare and Finance.

Each category will help to highlight unique challenges related to the industry and allows participants the chance to demonstrate their capabilities for some of the top software companies in the Montreal area.

FAQ

I don't have any experience in data analysis, can I still compete?

Most definitely! The competition is open to programmers of all levels. If you want to build some experience before the competition, checkout our Python workshop on Nov 8th!

What is the schedule?

The event will start Friday night with the opening ceremonies and a company networking event. You are then free from Friday night to Sunday morning to work on your project. Winners will be announced Sunday afternoon after judging!

Where is the event held?

The event will be entirely housed in the Trottier Building. We have booked out all the floors for you to hack away in peace!

Who can attend?

Any undergraduate or graduate student from McGill and surrounding area are welcome to participate!

Is there an entrance fee?

There will be a small ticket price that grants you entrance and a t-shirt and meals for the weekend!

What do I need to bring?

Your student ID and a laptop! And any chargers and whatever gets you through 36 hours of hacking!
